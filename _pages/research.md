My academic research mainly focuses on two main areas: Developing interpretable Machine Learning models and designing self-supervised learning algorithms. Due to the growing use of AI systems in highly sensitive areas, the need for explainability is unquestionable. My research lies in creating interpretable systems to enable the characterization of accuracy, fairness and transparency.

On the other hand, Most machine learning models rely on huge labeled data to learn and make decisions. Apparently, labeled data is more expensive and hard to get than we think. Hence, it should be possible to train machines with less labeled data and still obtain optimal performance.
