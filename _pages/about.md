 I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science at Iowa State University. In my research, I'm interested in Explainable Artificial Intelligence, Computer vision and Semi-supervised Learning. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Addis Ababa Science and Technology University in Ethiopia.
 
In the meantime, I work as a teaching assistant at the department of Computer Science. I have worked as a TA for  [COM S 227: Object Oriented Programming](https://catalog.iastate.edu/search/?P=COM%20S%20227) and quickly excelled in becoming a head TA.
