 I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science at Iowa State University. My research mainly focuses on Label-efficient machine learning algorithms for computer vision. I am also interested in enabling the interpretability of Machine Learning algorithms. I currently work in a research group led by my advisor Professor Wallapak Tavanapong.

 I have also been accepted as one of the 300 students across North America for Google's Computer Science Research Mentorship program. Since September 2022, I am being mentored by Dr. Boyu Wang who is a Researcher at Google.

 In the meantime, I work as a teaching assistant at the department of Computer Science. I am working as a head TA for  [COM S 227: Object Oriented Programming](https://catalog.iastate.edu/search/?P=COM%20S%20227) and quickly excelled in becoming a head TA.
 
 I earned my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Addis Ababa Science and Technology University in Ethiopia.
